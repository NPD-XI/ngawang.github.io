My Academic Journey

A look into my curiosity, projects, and skills

Curiosity as a Compass

I’ve always been an eager and curious learner. One teacher from my primary school even called me an **‘aggressive learner,’** which I now see as a funny and encouraging memory. This drive has pushed me to work hard and stay curious, making my academic journey a constant adventure in learning.

I believe that passion for learning is the first step towards innovation.

Key Projects & Achievements

Technology Digital Fabrication

My Fab Academy Journey

2024

My participation in the Fab Academy course was a transformative experience. For my final project, I created the **Japoe Pot**, a smart irrigation system that uses sensors to automatically water potted plants. It brought together my skills in electronics, programming, and digital fabrication to create a working prototype that simplifies plant care.

Innovation Public Speaking

Presenting at the Bhutan Innovation Forum (BIF)

October 2024

I was fortunate to present my Japoe Pot project at the BIF, an international summit curated to support His Majesty’s vision for the Gelephu Mindfulness City. It was an incredible opportunity to share my work and connect with Nobel laureates and global thought leaders from around the world.

Empathy Teamwork

Makeathon: Assistive Technology

2024-2025

Participating in Makeathon events taught me the power of technology to bring meaningful change. My team and I designed assistive writing tools for students with cerebral palsy, which helped them write and draw more comfortably. This experience highlighted the importance of empathy and collaboration.

Design Problem-Solving

Aresam Space Settlement Competition

2024

Working on this competition to design a large space settlement orbiting Mars gave me invaluable insights into large-scale problem-solving and futuristic design. Our team’s shared understanding of the challenges made this a unique and collaborative experience.
