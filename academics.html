My Academic Journey

Curiosity, Innovation, and Entrepreneurship

A Driven Learner

I’ve always been an **eager and curious learner**. One primary school teacher called me an "aggressive learner," which I now see as a funny and encouraging memory! This drive has pushed me to excel in my studies since primary school, encouraging me to keep working hard and remain curious in my learning.

A major highlight was receiving the **Certificate for Academics Topper from His Majesty the King of Bhutan**.

Digital Fabrication & Innovation

The Fab Academy and Japoe Pot

2024 | MIT-Affiliated Digital Fabrication Course

My interest in technology deepened through the six-month **Fab Academy** course, a hands-on digital fabrication program. My final project was the **Japoe Pot**, a smart irrigation system that uses sensors to monitor soil moisture and automatically water potted plants. This project successfully integrated my skills in **electronics, programming, and digital fabrication** into a working prototype.

Bhutan Innovation Forum (BIF)

October 2024 | International Summit Presentation

I was fortunate to present the Japoe Pot at the **Bhutan Innovation Forum (BIF)**, a summit supporting His Majesty’s vision for the Gelephu Mindfulness City. Presenting at this three-day event, which hosted **Nobel laureates, global thought leaders, and influential figures**, was an incredible opportunity to share my work and connect with innovators worldwide.

Makeathon: Assistive Technology

2024-2025 | Empathy and Collaboration

Participating in **Makeathon** events taught me the power of technology to bring meaningful change. My team and I designed assistive writing tools, like **grip holders and finger cushions**, using digital fabrication to support students with cerebral palsy. This experience profoundly highlighted the importance of **empathy and collaboration** in technology design.

Future Design & Aspirations

Aresam Space Settlement Competition

2024 | Large-Scale Systems Design

My team participated in the **Aresam Space Settlement Design Competition**, focused on designing a large space settlement orbiting Mars. Though we were selected for the semi-finals but couldn’t attend, the project gave me invaluable insights into **large-scale problem-solving and futuristic design**, reinforcing the value of shared understanding and teamwork.

Aspiration: AI Regulation

Goal for Future Study

I aspire to work in the field of **Artificial Intelligence (AI) regulation**. I believe it’s critical to ensure AI is developed **responsibly and ethically**. My interest is in guiding AI to serve humanity fairly, protecting values like safety, inclusivity, and well-being.

Entrepreneurship & Practical Skills

Bhutan Hydroponics Internship

Summer 2024 | Sustainable Agriculture

I interned at **Bhutan Hydroponics**, learning about soilless farming technologies and their potential to transform local agriculture. This experience showed me that **entrepreneurship** drives sustainable growth, creates employment, and encourages self-reliance in Bhutan. The best part was making fresh salads from the produce!

Stage Performance & Expression

Extracurricular Activity

I enjoy performing on stage, having taken part in several plays, including Shakespearean performances. This helps me step out of my comfort zone, build **creativity**, and find joy in connecting with audiences through storytelling.
