About Me

I was born in May 2008 in Paro, Bhutan. My family roots are in Punakha, but I spent most of my childhood in Paro because of my father’s work.

In 2020, I joined The Royal Academy as part of the COVID badge. Adjusting to the Academy’s holistic learning system was challenging, but I gradually discovered new ways of growing academically and personally.

Early experiences taught me adaptability, patience, and the importance of learning beyond textbooks.
