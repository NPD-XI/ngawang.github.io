Key Projects & Innovation

Technology & Fabrication

The Japoe Pot (Fab Academy)

2024 | Smart Irrigation System

My deepest dive into technology was through the six-month Fab Academy course. For my final project, I created the Japoe Pot, a smart irrigation system for potted plants. It uses sensors to monitor soil moisture and automatically waters the plant when needed. This project integrated electronics, programming, and digital fabrication into a fully working prototype.

Bhutan Innovation Forum (BIF)

October 2024 | International Presentation

I had the distinct honor of presenting the Japoe Pot at the Bhutan Innovation Forum. Curated to support the vision of the Gelephu Mindfulness City, this event allowed me to connect with Nobel laureates and global thought leaders, sharing my work on an international stage.

Makeathon: Assistive Tech

2024-2025 | Social Impact

Building on my fabrication skills, I participated in Makeathon events to design assistive technology for individuals with disabilities. My team created writing tools like grip holders and finger cushions to help students with cerebral palsy write more comfortably, teaching me the power of empathy in engineering.

Design & Future Thinking

Aresam Space Settlement

2024 | Systems Design Competition

I participated in the Aresam Space Settlement Design Competition, designing a large-scale habitat orbiting Mars. The project involved creating essential systems for housing, healthcare, and safety for thousands of residents. It provided invaluable insights into large-scale problem-solving and futuristic engineering.

Sustainable Entrepreneurship

Bhutan Hydroponics Internship

Summer 2024 | Soilless Farming

During my internship at Bhutan Hydroponics, I explored soilless farming technologies and their potential to transform local agriculture. Beyond the technical aspects, I realized how sustainable entrepreneurship can create employment and drive self-reliance in Bhutan.
