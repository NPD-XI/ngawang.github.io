About Me

I was born in May 2008 in Paro, though my family roots are in Punakha. Because of my father’s work, I spent most of my childhood in Paro. In 2020, I joined The Royal Academy as part of the COVID badge. Adjusting to the Academy’s holistic learning system was a challenge, but over time, I discovered new ways of growing not only academically but also personally.

My early life experiences taught me adaptability, patience, and the importance of learning beyond textbooks.

School Life

At The Royal Academy, learning goes far beyond classrooms. I learned science as a single subject to understand the bigger picture of the world. The Academy emphasizes five areas of development, which shaped me into a more balanced individual. Roadmaps, reflections, and exposure to different experiences helped me to gradually build emotional strength and a stronger identity.

Though it wasn’t easy at first, the practice of reflection became a turning point in how I approached learning and life itself.

Projects & Learning

Some of my most meaningful growth happened through projects. Completing the Fab Academy was one of my biggest milestones. My final project, the Japoe Pot, was a smart irrigation system designed for potted plants, combining technology and sustainability. I also participated in the Aresam Space Settlement Design Competition, where our team worked on futuristic community design orbiting Mars.

Sports & Activities

Sports have always been an important part of my life. I enjoyed basketball tournaments, morning physical training, and even had the chance to be part of an athletics exchange program in Japan. Sports taught me endurance, discipline, and teamwork. Beyond sports, I participated in farming activities, Buddhist retreats, robotics learning, and internships on entrepreneurship, which gave me valuable insights into the wider world.

Reflections

Looking back, my student journey has been filled with challenges, achievements, and growth. From adjusting to a new learning environment to working on international projects, every step has shaped who I am becoming. I learned that growth requires courage, reflection, and consistency.

My journey is far from over, and I look forward to continuing to learn, explore, and build experiences that shape both my present and my future.
